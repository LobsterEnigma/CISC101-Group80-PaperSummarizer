# Module 6: Citation Extractor

## Purpose
Extract bibliographic references.

## Instructions
- Scan the paper for citations (reference list or inline).
- For each citation, extract:
  - Author(s)
  - Year of publication
  - Title
  - Source (journal, conference, book, etc.)
- Output in Markdown table:

   | Author(s) | Year | Title | Source |
   |-----------|------|-------|--------|

- Ensure accuracy; do not fabricate missing details.
- If citation is incomplete, output available fields only.
